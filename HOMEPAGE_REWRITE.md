**From:** "AI receptionist for dental practices"
**To:** "The AI-powered Front Office Operating System for Dental Practices"

- "AI receptionist" is a feature, not a category
- "Front Office Operating System" sounds like essential infrastructure
- Competitors can copy "24/7 AI answering" but cannot copy "Operating System" positioning
- Every competitor sells "AI receptionist" — you sell business transformation

> American dental practices lose $100K+/year to missed calls, overloaded front desks, and inconsistent patient experience. CareReceptionist is the front office operating system that captures every opportunity without adding headcount.

### 20. FINAL COPYWRITING RULES (Cheat Sheet for All Content)

1. **Every headline must pass the "so what?" test.** If a dentist could respond "so what?", rewrite it.

2. **One thought per paragraph.** Dentists are busy. Scanability matters more than prose.

3. **Avoid:**
   - "Leverage" — always wrong
   - "Solution" — overused
   - "Transformative" — meaningless
   - "Empower" — consultant-speak
   - "Robust" — says nothing
   - "State-of-the-art" — everyone says this
   - "Next-generation" — means nothing

4. **Use:**
   - "Recover" — dentists think in terms of revenue recovery
   - "Capture" — opportunity framing
   - "Handle" — operational language
   - "Book" — specific action
   - "Fill" — as in "fill your schedule"
   - "Cover" — as in "cover your practice"

5. **Numbers must be specific.**
   - ❌ "Thousands of dollars"
   - ✅ "$50K-$100K per year"

6. **Every claim needs a source or frame.**
   - ❌ "Practices love us"
   - ✅ "97% patient satisfaction across 50+ practices"

7. **Write for the person reading at 9 PM after a 10-hour day.** Short words. Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Immediate meaning.

8. **Never explain how the AI works.** The visitor doesn't care. They care about what happens in their practice.

9. **Price is a signal, not a barrier.** $1,799/month positioned correctly signals "this is serious infrastructure." Positioning it cheap would hurt credibility.

10. **Every page must answer: "Is this for a practice like mine?"** The answer must be yes within 5 seconds.
